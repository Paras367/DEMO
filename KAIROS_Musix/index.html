THE ORIGIN

Born in the digital void, KAIROS isn't just a musician; they are a curator of frequencies. Blending the melancholic melodies of 80s synth-pop with the aggressive, distorted basslines of modern industrial, KAIROS creates a soundscape that feels like a memory from a future that hasn't happened yet.

With over 5 million streams and a cult following in Berlin and Tokyo, the mission is clear: To disrupt the silence.

"I don't make music to be background noise. I make music to be felt in your chest."
