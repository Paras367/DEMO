Culture of Tripura, Mizoram and Chandigarh

Introduction

India is a country with many cultures. Each state has its own traditions, food, festivals, and dances. In this project, we learn about the culture of Tripura, Mizoram, and Chandigarh.

Tripura

Tripura is a state in North-East India. Many tribal people live here. Hojagiri dance is very famous in Tripura. Rice and fish are common foods.

Mizoram

Mizoram is also in North-East India. The Bamboo Dance is very popular here. People wear colorful clothes. The state is known for its clean environment.

Chandigarh

Chandigarh is a modern city in India. It is well planned and clean. It is famous for the Rock Garden. Punjabi culture is seen here.
